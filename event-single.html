    Feed My Starving Children

    on 23 July 2020 at Ukrainian Community Center

    Every year, millions of children die from preventable causes such as pneumonia, diarrhea, malaria and undernutrition.

    Hunger is still causing nearly half of deaths in children under 5 years old. From this data, we estimate at least 6,200 children die each day from causes related to undernutrition.

    Feed My Starving Children (FMSC) FMSC meals are developed by food science and nutrition professionals to supplement nutritional needs and reduce problems with malnutrition. Nutrition allows children to grow, thrive and develop to their full potential.
